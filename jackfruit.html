JACKFRUIT BIRYANI

INGREDIENTS

ONIONS

3

CHILLIS

4

BUTTERMILK

1 GLASS

JACKFROOT

300 GMS

BASMATI

500 GMS

CURD

1 cup

GARLIC

6-7

CORIANDER SEED

1 CUP

STEPS

(80 MINUTES)

  • Cut the jackfroot into medium cube size and soak it in buttermik for 10mins.
  • In the mean while take a pan and add biryani spices each 2 roast on low flame.
  • Take the mixer and add 6-7 garlic,roasted biryani spices to it grind to smooth fine paste.
  • Take a stockpot add 4 tbsp oil,soaked jackfruit to it.Heat untill jackfroot becomes soft and light golden on low flame for 5 mins.
  • After 5 mins turn off the stove make it cool for some time.
  • Take another stockpot and add 2 tbsp oil,biryani spices each 2. Heat for 3mins on high flame.
  • After 3 mins add chopped thin slices onion,4 chillies to it. Allow it to cook for 4mins on low flame.
  • When turn to light brown color add sliced tomato,1 tbsp turmeric and mix it add grinded masala paste. Allow it to cook for 5mins.
  • Next add cooked jackfroot,buttermilk to it. Mix it gently and close the lid. Allow it to cook for 5mins.
  • Later add 500gms soaked basmati rice to the mixture and stir the mixture. Allow it to cook for 9mins on high flame.
  • At last add lemon juice and mix the mixture.
  • Turn of the stove and serve hot.

Kathal Biryani, also known as Jackfruit Biryani, is a dish from the Kayastha community's courtly cuisine. This community thought to be descended from Chitragupta, Yamraj's divine scribe, occupied prestigious administrative roles in royal courts throughout history.
