ZAFRANI BIRYANI


INGREDIENTS

CHICKEN

5 BREASTS

MINT

2 STEMS

GINGER

GARLIC

4 tbsp

CASHEW

10

ONIONS

3 ROUGHLY CHOPPED

WATER

2 CUPS

TURMERIC

1/4 SPOON

CURD

2 TBSP

GREEN CHILLI

3

BASMATI

1 KG

BIRYANI SPICES

EACH 3

SAFFRON

1/2 TBSP

STEPS

(120 MINUTES)

  • Soak the cashew for 2mins and add the to the mixer to make fine smooth paste.
  • Heat the milk and add 1/2 tbsp saffron to it.
  • Wash the chicken and cut into large peices.
  • Take the chicken in a bowl and add cashew paste,2tbsp of saffron milk,curd 2tbsp,garlic paste 4tbsp,turmeric 3/4tbsp,chilli poweder 2tbsp,garam masala 2tbsp,fried onions 5tbsp,coriander 1/2 cup,mint 1/2 cup,chilli 2,lemon 1 and mix it entirely.
  • Mariante for 90mins.
  • Soak the basmati rice for 30mins.
  • Take 1.5 litres of water add biryani spices each-3,salt 1.5 tbsp and heat it in a soakpot.
  • After heating add Ghee 1 tbsp,soaked rice,cook upto 8mins on high heat.
  • Cook till 3/4th done.
  • Greace a pan with 1 tbsp oil and add marinated chicken,4 tbsp of hot ghee on top mix the chicken and close the lid cook for 5mins on medium heat.
  • After 5mins give a stir and cook for 20mins on low heat.
  • After 20mins turn of heat and add rice on top,add remaining saffron milk,2tbsp of fried onion and cook for 8mins on low heat.
  • After 8mins mix the rice and chicken gently.
  • Serve Hot

The Zafrani Biryani has a history of its kind and originated in in the imperial kitchens of the Nizam's. The primary ingredient or the hero of this dish is saffron (which is “zafran” in persian and arabic) and so it’s been named. .

This authentic Biryani from the City of Hyderabad is something to reckon with. It has subtle flavours and aroma due to the special spice which is Saffron. Not only does it give this Biryani a unique flavour but also imparts an excellent colour and hue. This makes it stand apart from the rest.
